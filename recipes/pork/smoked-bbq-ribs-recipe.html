'Competition Style' Smoked BBQ Pork Ribs Recipe

January 15, 2019

'Competition Style' Smoked BBQ Pork Ribs

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 6 hours
Yields: 4 Servings
For Beginners

01.

Remove membrane lengthwise from back of ribs (use a paper towel if necessary). Pat ribs dry. Cover ribs with rub on both sides.

02.

Prep smoker for cooking at 225 degrees fahrenheit. I recommend using apple, cherry, or hickory wood.

03.

Place ribs on smoker and cook for 3 hours. Optionally spray with apple juice every hour.

04.

After 3 hours have passed, spray ribs a final time with apple juice. Wrap ribs in aluminum foil and place back on smoker. Continue to cook for 2 more hours.

05.

Unwrap ribs from aluminum foil. Brush with favorite BBQ sauce (try to avoid the cheap brands). Place ribs back on smoker.

06.

Continue to smoke ribs for 1 hour, or until BBQ has caramelized. Remove from smoker and brush with more BBQ sauce if desired. Serve hot.
